# Bloodaxe
*Weapon ([greataxe](2-Mechanics/CLI/items/greataxe.md)), very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d12 S
- **Properties**: [Heavy](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic axe. The axe deals an extra `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) necrotic damage to creatures that aren't constructs or undead. If you reduce such a creature to 0 hit points with an attack using this axe, you gain 10 temporary hit points.

This axe is forged from a dark, rust-colored metal and once belonged to the goliath barbarian Grog Strongjaw of Vox Machina.

*Source: Explorer's Guide to Wildemount p. 266*
