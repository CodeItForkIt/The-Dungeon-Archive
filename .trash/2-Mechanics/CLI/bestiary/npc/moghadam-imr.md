## Traits

***Special Equipment.*** Moghadam wields [Ruinblade](2-Mechanics/CLI/items/ruinblade-imr.md) (see appendix C).

***Artificial Mind.*** Moghadam can manifest an artificial mind as a floating spectral image of a [demilich](2-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/undead/demilich.md)'s jeweled skull. Moghadam can communicate telepathically with this mind, send it up to 300 feet away from him, and see and hear through it.

***Infoportation (1/Day).*** Moghadam can teleport himself into an unoccupied space next to his artificial mind.

***Spellcasting.*** Moghadam is an 18th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, `dice:1d20+8|noform|text(+8)` to hit with spell attacks). He has the following artificer spells prepared:

**Cantrips (at will)**: [dancing lights](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/dancing-lights.md), [light](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/light.md), [message](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/message.md), [shocking grasp](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/shocking-grasp.md)

**1st level (4 slots)**: [comprehend languages](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/comprehend-languages.md), [cure wounds](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/cure-wounds.md), [grease](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/grease.md), [identify](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/identify.md)

**2nd level (3 slots)**: [detect thoughts](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts.md), [invisibility](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/invisibility.md), [heat metal](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/heat-metal.md)

**3rd level (3 slots)**: [dispel magic](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/dispel-magic.md), [flame arrows](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/flame-arrows-xge.md), [hypnotic pattern](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/hypnotic-pattern.md)

**4th level (3 slots)**: [fabricate](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/fabricate.md), [stone shape](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/stone-shape.md), [stoneskin](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/stoneskin.md)

**5th level (1 slots)**: [creation](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/creation.md)

## Actions

***Ruinblade.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `dice:1d20+6|noform|text(+6)` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* `dice:2d6+2|noform|avg|text(9)` (`2d6 + 2`) slashing damage.

***Blight (1/Day).*** While wielding [Ruinblade](2-Mechanics/CLI/items/ruinblade-imr.md), Moghadam can cast the [blight](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/blight.md) spell (DC 15).

***Disintegrate (1/7 Days).*** While wielding [Ruinblade](2-Mechanics/CLI/items/ruinblade-imr.md), Moghadam can cast the [disintegrate](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/disintegrate.md) spell against any nonmagical object or creation of magical force.

***Information Overload (Recharge 5-6).*** Moghadam overloads the thoughts of one creature within 5 feet of either himself or his artificial mind. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw or take `dice:4d8+4|noform|avg|text(22)` (`4d8 + 4`) psychic damage, and the next attack roll made against the target before the end of Moghadam's next turn has advantage.
