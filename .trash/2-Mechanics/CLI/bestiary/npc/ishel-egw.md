## Traits

***Fey Ancestry.*** Ishel has advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Charmed), and magic can't put Ishel to sleep.

***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, Ishel has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom ([Perception](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on sight.

***Innate Spellcasting.*** Ishel's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 11). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

**At will**: [dancing lights](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/dancing-lights.md)

**1/day each**: [darkness](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/darkness.md), [faerie fire](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/faerie-fire.md)
