# Oil of Taggit
*Adventuring gear, poison (contact)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](TTRPG/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 400 gp

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](TTRPG/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 24 hours. The [poisoned](TTRPG/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [unconscious](TTRPG/rules/conditions.md#Unconscious). The creature wakes up if it takes damage.

*Source: SRD 5.1*
