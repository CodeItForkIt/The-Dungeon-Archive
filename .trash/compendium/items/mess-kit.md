# Mess Kit
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 2 sp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This tin box contains a cup and simple cutlery. The box clamps together, and one side can be used as a cooking pan and the other as a plate or shallow bowl.

*Source: SRD 5.1 and the Basic Rules (2014)*
