# Dragongleam
*Weapon ([spear](Mechanics/items/spear.md))*  

- **Damage**:

  - Two-handed: 1d8 P
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Thrown](Mechanics/Rules/item-properties.md#Thrown), [Versatile](Mechanics/Rules/item-properties.md#Versatile)
